How HerSafe Ride Works

Curious about how HerSafe Ride ensures your safety? Let us give you a glimpse into our seamless process. Your peace of mind is our mission.

1

Download the App

Get started by downloading the HerSafe Ride app from the App Store or Google Play Store. It's free and easy to use

4

Track Your Ride

Once your ride is confirmed, you can track the location of your driver in real-time on the app. You'll receive updates on their arrival time, Sit back, and enjoy the ride.

Sign Up

Create your HerSafe Ride account and provide some basic information to get started. Don't worry; we keep your personal information safe and secure

Make Your Payment

Our professional driver will ensure you reach your destination safely and on time. Make payment with your card or transfer.
