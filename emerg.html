Emergency Assistance with HerSafe Ride

Your safety is paramount at HerSafe Ride

How It Works

In case of an emergency during a ride, simply tap the dedicated Emergency Assistance button within the app. This immediately notifies our support team, who will reach out to you and coordinate necessary assistance

Medical Emergencies

For medical emergencies, our support team will guide you through the necessary steps, including contacting emergency services and providing assistance until help arrives

Unexpected Situations

Whether it's a breakdown, a feeling of unease, or any unexpected situation, our team is ready to assist. Your safety and comfort are our top priorities.

Driver Verification

In situations where you feel uncertain about your driver's identity, our Emergency Assistance feature includes driver verification protocols to ensure your security

Real-Time Support

Our support team is available in real-time to address your concerns and coordinate with local authorities if needed. Your safety is our commitment, and we're here for you 24/7

Stay Safe with Us

HerSafe Ride is dedicated to providing a secure and reliable transportation experience. With our Emergency Assistance feature, travel with confidence, knowing that support is just a tap away.

Get the Assistance You Need

If you ever need assistance or have questions, our Help Center is here to guide you.
