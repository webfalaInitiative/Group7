FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a ride?

To book a ride, simply open our app, enter your pickup and drop-off locations, choose your preferred vehicle option, and confirm your ride. It's that easy!

Can I request a female driver?

Yes, you can request a female driver. Our app allows you to specify your preference for a female driver when booking your ride. Simply select the option in the app, and we'll do our best to accommodate your request.

What safety measures are in place for passengers?

Your safety is paramount. We verify our drivers, provide real-time tracking, and offer 24/7 emergency assistance. Rest assured, we prioritize your well-being every step of the way.

What happens in case of an emergency during a ride?

Our app includes an Emergency Assistance feature. Tap the button, and our support team will assist you in real-time, coordinating with local authorities if necessary
