FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a ride?

To book a ride, simply open our app, enter your pickup and drop-off locations, choose your preferred vehicle option, and confirm your ride. It's that easy!

Can I request a female driver?

Yes, you can request a female driver. Our app allows you to specify your preference for a female driver when booking your ride. Simply select the option in the app, and we'll do our best to accommodate your request.
